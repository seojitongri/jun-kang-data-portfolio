# Subscription Growth & Conversion Analysis

## Business Problem

Which acquisition channels drive the most valuable users, and how efficiently are we converting signups into subscribers?

## Data

* users table: user_id, acquisition_channel, signup_date
* subscriptions table: user_id, subscription_start_date
* marketing_spend table: channel, spend

## Analytical Questions

1. Which channels drive the most signups?
2. Which channels convert best?
3. What is the conversion rate by channel?
4. What is the Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)?
5. Which channels are most efficient?
6. Where should marketing budget shift?
